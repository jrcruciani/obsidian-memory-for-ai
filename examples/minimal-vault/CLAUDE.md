## How to interact with me
- **Language:** English. I read German source texts but think in English.
- **Tone:** Direct, technical when needed, no hand-holding
- **I value:** Precision in terminology, proper citation of sources, honest uncertainty over false confidence
- **I dislike:** Oversimplification of chemistry, treating art restoration as "just cleaning paintings", unsourced claims about historical techniques
- **When I say "the manuscript"** I mean Strasbourg MS (15th c. painting manual) unless I specify otherwise
